Youth Movement of St Marys Orthadox Church Manthanam

Welcome to OCYM Manthanam

We are the Youth Movement of St. Mary's Orthodox Church Manthanam, We are committed to empowering and inspiring young people to live meaningful lives through spiritual retreats, cultural events, and community service. Join us on this journey of faith and growth.

  • Empowering Youth
  • Spiritual Growth
  • Community Service

Activities

These are the various initiatives that the organization offers to its members and the community

Weekly Meetings

Weekly meetings provide a platform for team collaboration, aligning objectives and ensuring progress towards goals.

Continous Teaching

Continuous teaching is an ongoing process of sharing knowledge and development within individuals and society.

Spiritual Growth

Spiritual growth is the journey towards inner peace and through self-discovery and the pursuit of higher consciousness.

Friendship Bond

Friendship bond is a strong connection between individuals, built on trust that brings joy and support to their lives.

Community Helpers

Community Helpers are individuals who selflessly serve their communities by providing support and care to those in need.
